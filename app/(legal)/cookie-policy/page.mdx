# Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

## What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience and allow certain features to work.

## How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

### Essential Cookies
These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation and access to secure areas of the website.

### Analytics Cookies
We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. These cookies help us improve our service by collecting anonymous information about:
- Pages visited
- Time spent on site
- How you arrived at our site
- Clicks and interactions

### Preference Cookies
These cookies remember your settings and preferences, such as:
- Language preference
- Theme preference (light/dark mode)
- Comment style preferences

### Marketing Cookies
We may use cookies to show you relevant advertisements about our services on other websites.

## Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services that appear on our pages:
- **Google Analytics**: For website analytics
- **Google AdSense**: For displaying advertisements
- **Social Media**: If you share our content

## Managing Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can:
- Delete all cookies that are already on your computer
- Set your browser to prevent cookies from being placed
